Why Donate?
Governments aren't set up to protect everyone who needs it. That's why we are here for every child & young person to protect them & stand by them in the face of injustice. But we can't do it alone. Your donations help us fight those injustices that cause so much sufferings in the world.

Together, with your support, we can improve conditions for those who are wronged, underprivileged & less fortunate.

Miles2Smile is a non-profit organization registered under Section-8 of the Companies Act 2013 (18 of 2013) and Rule 8-Companies (Incorporation) Rules, 2014. The organization has its headquarter
in New Delhi. The prime working area of the organization is relief & rehabilitation of distressed individuals or groups and educational upliftment of the marginalized and underprivileged.
